This website was created by Nor Fatihah Binti Eshad as part of the IML470 course at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM). It is a creative and educational platform where users can explore various artworks and learn from the curated tutorials provided.

I created this website not only to meet the assignment requirements but also to express my passion for the arts and my interest in building digital platforms that are informative and visually engaging.

This website reflects my belief that libraries are not just places for books, but also vibrant hubs where creativity and information come together. Here, you will find curated artworks, engaging tutorials, and insightful resources meant to inspire students and art enthusiasts alike.

Outside of my academic life, I enjoy reading, discovering digital art, and learning about different cultures. I believe that continuous learning and creative expression are key to personal growth. Through the FAA Library, I aim to offer a calm and elegant online space where people can enjoy, learn, and share their appreciation for the arts.

I hope this platform provides a meaningful experience and encourages everyone especially fellow students to embrace both knowledge and creativity in their own journeys. Thank you for visiting!

The development of FAA Library has helped enhance my understanding of HTML, CSS, design principles, and user experience. It also allowed me to express creativity through the design and content of each section of the site.

I hope this website serves as an inspiring and informative space for art lovers, students, and anyone seeking to learn more about art techniques and appreciation. All content, including tutorials, artwork curation, and design, was developed for educational purposes under the Faculty of Information Management.
