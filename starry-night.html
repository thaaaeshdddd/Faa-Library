Starry Night

Description: "The Starry Night" by Vincent van Gogh, painted in 1889, is a mesmerizing portrayal of a night sky swirling with energy above the quiet village of Saint-Rémy. With intense blues and vibrant yellows, the painting captures Van Gogh’s inner turmoil and awe for the cosmos. The rolling hills, cypress tree, and glowing moon create a dreamlike scene that transcends reality. This masterpiece symbolizes the artist's emotional depth and his quest for peace through art, despite the chaos in his mind.
