Glitch Girl - Digital Fragmentation

Glitch Girl explores the beauty found in digital distortion. Through flickering lines, pixelated colors, and fragmented shapes, the artwork expresses the idea of identity lost in a digital world ‚Äî both mysterious and mesmerizing.

The glitch technique, often seen as an error in technology, becomes an intentional style in this artwork. The girl's face appears to flicker in and out of reality, hinting at the fragile boundary between real and virtual, human and machine.

This digital piece invites reflection on how we present ourselves online, where image and identity are constantly being edited, filtered, and reassembled. Glitch Girl challenges viewers to embrace imperfection as a new aesthetic.
