Introduction

Digital art blends creativity with technology. Whether you're into character design, digital painting, or concept art, this tutorial will help you understand tools, techniques, and styles to boost your skills.

Tools of the Trade üíª

  • Digital Tablet (e.g., Wacom, Huion, iPad with Apple Pencil)
  • Software like Adobe Photoshop, Procreate, Clip Studio Paint
  • Stylus and pressure-sensitive pen
  • Color calibration for accurate tones

Fundamentals to Learn üé®

  • Layers: Work in layers for flexibility and control.
  • Brush Settings: Learn to customize brushes for line work, shading, and textures.
  • Color Theory: Use complementary colors and blending modes.
  • Lighting & Shadows: Create depth and mood through value contrast.

üí° Pro Tips:

  • Use reference images to improve anatomy and lighting.
  • Try gesture drawing to loosen up and build confidence.
  • Save iterations and work in high resolution.
  • Join art communities for feedback and improvement.

Practice Task üß™

Create a fantasy character from scratch. Sketch it in rough form, build up shapes with color layers, add details, and finalize it with effects or lighting. Share your work for critique!
